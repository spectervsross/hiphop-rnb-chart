June weather strategy

  • A 30% rain forecast often means brief, heavy showers rather than an all-day washout.
  • Use mornings for the Peak, ferry, waterfront or beach.
  • Use afternoons for malls, cafés and late lunches.
  • Carry a compact umbrella, SPF 50, tissues and very light clothes.

What to skip

  • Hiking anywhere.
  • Big Buddha and Ngong Ping unless it is a major priority.
  • Luxury malls except as air-conditioned shelter.
  • Hotel rooftop bars and premium cocktail temples.

Five practical tips

These reduce heat, walking and unnecessary friction.

Get an Octopus cardUse it for the MTR, buses, ferries and convenience stores.
Use short taxisThey are worth it for station-to-beach and late-night returns.
Carry cashMarkets and older local restaurants may prefer it.
Start earlyGo out around 9–10 a.m. and rest indoors after lunch.
Check opening hoursPopular local places may close early or keep irregular days off.
